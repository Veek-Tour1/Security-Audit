# Recommendations & Roadmap

## Phase 1: Critical (0-3 months)
1. **Implement data encryption** for all citizen records
2. **Deploy role-based access control** 
3. **Establish backup procedures** for geospatial data

## Phase 2: High Priority (3-6 months)
4. **Deploy intrusion detection system**
5. **Implement multi-factor authentication**
6. **Develop formal incident response plan**

## Expected Outcomes
- Achieve NDPR compliance within 6 months
- Reduce risk score from 8/10 to 3/10
- Protect sensitive citizen and land data
